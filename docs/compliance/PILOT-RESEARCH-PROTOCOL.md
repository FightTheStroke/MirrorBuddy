## 1. Study Objective

Evaluate the impact of MirrorBuddy (AI-assisted study kit) on the autonomy and cognitive load of students with ADHD and Dyslexia.

## 3. Methodology

- **Participants:** 20 students (8-14 years old) with certified neurodivergence.
- **Duration:** 4 weeks of active usage.
- **Tools:** MirrorBuddy "Astuccio" (Pomodoro, Mind Maps, Voice AI).

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

1. **Autonomy Index:** Decrease in "Help Requests" to parents/teachers.
2. **Focus Quality:** Stability of Pomodoro cycles.
3. **Engagement:** Depth and frequency of AI-Maestro interactions.
