## Complaint Procedures

### AI-Specific Complaints (AgID)

1. Submit via PEC to protocollo@pec.agid.gov.it
2. Include: system description, alleged non-compliance, evidence
3. AgID may request additional documentation within 30 days
4. Expected response: To be verified (no published SLA as of 2025)

### Data Protection Complaints (Garante)

1. Online form at https://www.garanteprivacy.it/home/diritti/come-agire-per-tutelare-i-propri-dati-personali
2. Or via PEC/registered mail with identity document
3. Response time: Typically 60–90 days for initial acknowledgment
4. Garante may issue binding decisions, fines, or corrective measures
