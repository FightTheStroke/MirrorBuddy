## Official AI Regulatory Authorities for Italy

**Primary AI Regulatory Authority in Italy**

- **Official Name**: Agenzia per l'Italia Digitale (Digital Italy Agency)
- **Role**: Enforces AI Act compliance (L.132/2025 implementation of EU AI Act)
- **Website**: https://www.agid.gov.it
- **Email**: protocollo@agid.gov.it
- **Phone**: +39 06 8526 1
- **Address**: Via della Transatlantica 31, 00144 Rome, Italy
- **Department for AI**: AI Policy Unit (Ufficio Politiche IA)

## Regulatory Compliance Framework

### High-Risk AI Classification (MirrorBuddy)

MirrorBuddy's AI tutor system is classified as HIGH-RISK under L.132/2025:

| Criterion                                | Status                |
| ---------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Generates educational content for minors | YES - High-risk       |
| Influences learning path decisions       | YES - High-risk       |
| Processes sensitive data (disabilities)  | YES - High-risk       |
| Could create discriminatory outcomes     | Potential - High-risk |

### Mandatory Contacts Before Launch

Before making MirrorBuddy generally available in Italy, document:

1. **AgID Pre-notification**
   - Submit technical dossier
   - Obtain AI Act compliance acknowledgment
   - Register in AI system registry

2. **Garante Consultation** (recommended)
   - Request guidance on data protection practices
   - Confirm GDPR compliance approach
   - Discuss children's data safeguards

## Contact Procedures

### For Compliance Questions

| **Question Type**          | **Contact**                  | **Response Time**   |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------- |
| AI Act compliance          | AgID (ai.policy@agid.gov.it) | 10-15 business days |
| Data protection (GDPR)     | Garante (garante@gpdp.it)    | 15-20 business days |
| Breach notification        | Garante emergency line       | 2-4 hours           |
| Children's data protection | Garante AI unit              | 5-10 business days  |

### For Incident Reporting

- **Data breach**: Contact Garante within 72 hours
- **AI safety incident**: Contact AgID AI Policy Unit immediately
- **Bias/discrimination complaint**: Contact Garante or AgID
