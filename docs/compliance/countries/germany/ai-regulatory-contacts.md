## State-Level Authorities

Note: Germany has 16 state-level data protection authorities (Landesdatenschutzbeauftragte).
For educational services, the relevant authority depends on where the student/school is located.
Federal BfDI handles cross-state and federal-level matters.

## Complaint Procedures

### AI Complaints (BNetzA)

1. Contact via email or postal mail
2. Include: AI system identification, alleged non-compliance, supporting evidence
3. BNetzA may conduct market surveillance investigations
4. Expected response: To be verified (procedures under development as of 2025)

### Data Protection Complaints (BfDI)

1. Online complaint form: https://www.bfdi.bund.de/DE/Service/Beschwerden/beschwerden_node.html
2. Or by postal mail / email to BfDI address above
3. Include: description of data processing, complaint details, prior contact with operator
4. Expected response: Acknowledgment within 3 months (GDPR Art. 78 deadline)
5. BfDI can issue corrective measures, fines, or refer to state authority

### Rights Exercise

1. Contact MirrorBuddy DPO first: roberdan@fightthestroke.org
2. If no satisfactory response within 30 days, file complaint with BfDI or relevant state authority
