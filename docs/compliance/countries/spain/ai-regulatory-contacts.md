## Complaint Procedures

### AI Complaints (AESIA)

1. AESIA is still establishing complaint procedures (as of 2025)
2. Interim: complaints may be directed to AEPD for data-related AI issues
3. Expected: formal complaint mechanism via aesia.gob.es once operational
4. Include: AI system description, alleged non-compliance, evidence

### Data Protection Complaints (AEPD)

1. Online complaint form: https://sedeagpd.gob.es/sede-electronica-web/vistas/formReclamacion/reclamacion.jsf
2. Electronic certificate or Cl@ve required for online submission
3. Or by postal mail to AEPD address above
4. Include: identity document, description of facts, evidence, prior contact with operator
5. Expected response: Acknowledgment within 30 days; investigation may take 6–12 months

### Rights Exercise

1. Contact MirrorBuddy DPO first: roberdan@fightthestroke.org
2. If no satisfactory response within 30 days, file complaint with AEPD
3. AEPD can issue warnings, fines (up to €20M or 4% global turnover), or corrective measures
