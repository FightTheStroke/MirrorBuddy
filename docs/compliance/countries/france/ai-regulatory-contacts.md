Same as above — **CNIL** is both the data protection and AI oversight authority in France.

## Complaint Procedures

### AI and Data Protection Complaints (CNIL)

1. Online complaint form: https://www.cnil.fr/fr/plaintes
2. Or by postal mail to CNIL address above
3. Include: description of AI system, nature of complaint, evidence, prior contact with operator
4. CNIL acknowledges receipt and may request additional information
5. Expected response: Initial acknowledgment within 2 months; investigation timeline varies

### Rights Exercise

1. Contact MirrorBuddy DPO first: roberdan@fightthestroke.org
2. If no satisfactory response within 30 days, file complaint with CNIL
3. CNIL can issue warnings, injunctions, or fines up to 4% of global turnover (GDPR)
