## English

### App Store Description

MirrorBuddy is an AI-powered learning companion designed specifically for children with disabilities, including cerebral palsy and learning difficulties. Created by FightTheStroke Foundation, MirrorBuddy provides personalized educational support through engaging, accessible interactions.

**Key Features:**

- AI-powered personalized learning adapted to each child's abilities
- Voice interaction with speech recognition optimized for children
- Visual exercises designed for motor skill development
- Progress tracking for parents and therapists
- Multi-sensory learning approach
- Safe, ad-free environment designed for children
- Offline mode for learning anywhere

**Designed for Inclusion**

MirrorBuddy was created with input from therapists, educators, and families to ensure it meets the unique needs of children with disabilities. Every feature is designed with accessibility in mind.

**Privacy & Safety**

Your child's privacy is our priority. MirrorBuddy is COPPA compliant and does not share data with third parties.
